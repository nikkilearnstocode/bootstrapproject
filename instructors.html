Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first yoga class?
For your first class, we recommend bringing a yoga mat, a water bottle, and comfortable clothing that allows for a full range of motion. If you don't have a yoga mat, don't worry - we have mats available for rent or purchase at the studio.
I'm a complete beginner. Which class should I start with?
We welcome yogis of all levels, including beginners! Our Soul Starter class is specifically designed for those new to yoga, focusing on foundational postures and breath work in a supportive and inclusive environment.
How early should I arrive before class?
Arriving at least 10-15 minutes before your class starts is a good practice. This gives you enough time to check in, prepare your space, and start the class feeling relaxed and not rushed.
Do you offer any workshops or events for deepening my yoga practice?
Yes, we frequently host workshops and special events aimed at deepening your practice and expanding your understanding of yoga. Topics range from advanced postures and meditation techniques to yoga philosophy and wellness. Keep an eye on our website and social media for upcoming events!
What are the benefits of becoming a SoulFlow Yoga Studio member?
Members enjoy numerous benefits, including unlimited access to our regular classes, priority booking, discounts on workshops and special events, and access to exclusive members-only sessions. Our membership plans are designed to support and inspire your yoga journey, providing you with the flexibility and community support to grow your practice.
