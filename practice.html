Drop-In Bliss

Perfect for those who prefer flexibility or are new to yoga, our Drop-In Bliss option allows you to join any single class of your choice, space permitting. This is a great way to experience different styles of yoga and instructors to find what best suits your needs.

Soul Flow Monthly

Our most popular plan, the Flow Membership, offers unlimited access to all regular classes, ensuring you can practice as often as you like. Enjoy the convenience of priority booking to secure your spot in your favorite classes and receive a 10% discount on all workshops and special events, making it easier to deepen your practice.

Eternal Soul Package

Designed for the dedicated yogi, the Eternal Soul Package encompasses all the benefits of the Flow Membership with the addition of exclusive perks. Enjoy a private session each month with your preferred instructor to personalize your practice, and take advantage of special discounts on retreats, creating space for deeper exploration and relaxation.
